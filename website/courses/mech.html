Course Overview

The B.E Mechanical Engineering program is designed to impart cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills required in today's dynamic industry. With a focus on innovation and research, this course transforms students into visionary professionals.

Our curriculum is constantly updated in collaboration with industry experts to ensure that our graduates are always a step ahead. From foundational concepts to advanced specializations, the program offers a holistic educational experience.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed 10+2 (or equivalent) examination.
  • Minimum aggregate of 60% in Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics).
  • Valid score in National/State level entrance examination.
  • For PG programs, a relevant Bachelor's degree with 55% marks is required.

Admission Process

  1. Submit the online application form with required documents.
  2. Appear for the entrance examination (if applicable).
  3. Attend the counseling / personal interview round.
  4. Document verification and fee payment.

Course Curriculum

Semester-wise structure for 4 Years duration.

Semester 1
  • Engineering Mathematics I
  • Engineering Physics
  • Basics of Electrical Engineering
  • Communication Skills
Semester 2
  • Engineering Mathematics II
  • Engineering Chemistry
  • Programming in C/C++
  • Engineering Graphics
Semester 3 to Final

Detailed specialization subjects, electives, mini-projects, and a final year major project.
